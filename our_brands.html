PURANI DILLI

At the KDA Sweets Purani Dilli restaurant we look to satiate your craving for authentic Delhi style vegetarian cuisine with fresh, high quality, mouth-watering food. Our popular, quick service restaurant is a delightful addition to the culinary scene of Bangalore because we have innovatively recreated Purani Dilli for you, both visually and in taste. Our interiors replicate the look and feel of Chandni Chowk while you dig into Chaats and thalis that have been prepared by trained chefs from Delhi. Our chefs not only source each authentic spice from the local markets of Delhi but also pay careful attention to recreating the flavors. Using these authentic masalas and methods of preparation that have been passed down through generations, they lovingly make the food that you crave. Whether you want a quick bite of aaloo tikki chaat, gol gappe, chhole bhature or a full fledged north indian thali, Purani Dilli is a must visit for every foodie.

 

CHEENI KUM

 

 

Guided by the master Indian chefs at KDA sweets, Cheeni kum is the namkeen, chulbuli sister concern that bridges the gap between tradition and contemporary Indian cuisine. We bring to you authentic, flavorful snacks on the go that suit the current Indian lifestyle. What ever be the occasion, an evening snack, an elaborate dinner, a tradition meal or a cocktail evening, Cheeni Kum has something for everybody. Snacking shouldn't be a guilty pleasure. We bring to you healthy wholesome chatpatte snacks from all over the country under one roof. Indulge yourself!

Our beautifully designed boxes also make these chocolates a delightful gift, that is, if you can give them away. Come pamper your senses with Amadora!
